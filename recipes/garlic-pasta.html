Garlic Pasta

Description

This quick Garlic Pasta with olive oil and Parmesan is a beautiful reminder that an elegant and flavorful dinner doesnâ€™t have to be complex or time-consuming.

Ingredients

  1. Pasta
  2. Olive Oil
  3. Garlic
  4. Parmesean
  5. Parsley
  6. Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Toss peeled garlic cloves with olive oil in a baking dish.
  2. Roast until garlic is light golden brown.
  3. Cook pasta, reserving 1 1/2 cups of the pasta cooking liquid.
  4. Blend the garlic with Parmesan and olive oil.
  5. Simmer the garlic mixture in a skillet with the reserved pasta water to thicken.
  6. Toss the drained pasta with remaining cheese and parsley. Serve warm topped with additional Parmesan. ENJOY!
