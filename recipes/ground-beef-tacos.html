Ground Beef Tacos

Description

Ground Beef Tacos is one of our favorite weeknight family meals, especially on Taco Tuesdays! I have a quick homemade taco seasoning that I love to use for this recipe so I can cook the taco meat in 10 minutes. Give it a try and itâ€™ll be your new go-to for taco night too!

Ingredients

  1. 1 teaspoon olive oil
  2. 1 pound lean ground beef
  3. 2 teaspoons chili powder
  4. 2 teaspoons cumin
  5. 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  6. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  7. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  9. 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  10. 1/2 cup water
  11. 8 corn or flour tortillas
  12. lettuce
  13. shredded Mexican cheese blend or cheddar cheese
  14. tomatoes
  15. chopped red onions

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in skillet over medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Drain any fat.
  2. Add the chili powder, cumin, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, pepper, tomato paste and water. Stir to combine and continue cooking over medium-low heat until the sauce has thickened, about 3-5 minutes
  3. Serve warm over tortillas with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and red onions, or your other desired toppings.
